# Top Words — English & German Exam Vocabulary Trainer

A static web app for practising the **most frequent English and German exam
vocabulary** across every CEFR level (**A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2**). It is built
for Turkish students preparing for **YDS, YÖKDİL, ÜDS (UDSP), KPDS, TOEFL, telc
and CEFR** exams, and also covers English **phrasal verbs** and German separable
verbs (**Partikelverben**). It includes flashcards, a searchable word list, and
**vocabulary exams of 20 questions each** with a final score shown when you
submit.
